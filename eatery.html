Cornetti

Italian Pastry. Known as "The Italian Croissant"

A Crescent shaped with buttery and flaky texture. It plays an integral part of the italian breakfast culture having it alongside coffee. Common in cafes and Pastry shops across Rome.

  • Ingredients: the dough is made with sugar, butter, salt, flour and yeast. It is then rolled and folded to create layers. flavourings is very flexible.
  • types: There are many different types of cornetti's served in Italy. Due to Cornetti's flexiblity, you can even have it plain. Some common fillings are crema pasticcera (pastry cream), chocolate jam and so on. The Cornetti can be also be glazed or powdered.
