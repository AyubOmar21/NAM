NAMSA

Non-aligned movement solidarity archive

Overview

NAMSA is an archive of materials collected by Olja Džuverović, a political worker engaged with the liberation movements of African nations and Yugoslavia. This archive provides a political and personal perspective on the Non-Aligned Movement (NAM), serving as a valuable tool for researching a complex and often trivialised movement!
